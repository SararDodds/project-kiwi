Field teams — technicians
  • Raise requests in Touchstone on site instead of relying on others
  • Complete work against fewer, larger work orders (not daily jobs)
  • Record all visits, time, and notes against the same work order
  • No "Take Ownership" — work is assigned to you directly
  • If work changes, raise a new request or contact Service Desk
  • Some transition work may be managed via paper or spreadsheets
  • System is similar to today, but with more structured workflows
Supervisors & forepersons
  • Work is assigned to individuals, not owner groups
  • You are responsible for reassigning work (e.g. leave coverage)
  • Service requests drive KPIs, not individual work orders
  • Critical jobs must be closed quickly and re-raised if needed
  • Less flexibility to edit work — errors may require new work orders
  • Greater visibility via Power BI reporting
  • Delegation of Authority (DOA) now sits with Service Delivery Managers
Admin & service desk
  • All work orders are created via Service Desk
  • Subcontractor work orders now auto-generate POs in NAV
  • Materials and consumables are managed directly in NAV
  • During transition, you will manage manual spreadsheets for open work
  • Responsible for closing legacy work orders in MaxiCloud
  • Apply Schedule of Rates (SOR) to make work billable
  • Reporting shifts from Start Centres to Power BI + saved queries
Managers & SDMs
  • Approval authority is centralised (DOA) at SDM level
  • KPIs are measured at the service request level
  • Improved visibility through Power BI reporting
  • More consistent processes across contracts and regions
  • Focus on performance, compliance, and data quality
  • Oversight of transition, workload balancing, and issue resolution during hypercare
While the systems and processes are changing, the work we do and our service commitments remain the same.
